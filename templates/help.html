Help & Support

Find answers to common questions about our platform.

LocalConnect24x7 is a platform that connects you with your favorite local stores. You can browse products from various shops in your area, add them to your cart, and place an order. We then facilitate a quick and reliable delivery right to your doorstep, 24/7.

We pride ourselves on 24/7 delivery. However, delivery times can vary slightly based on the shop's operational hours and your location. Each shop sets its own delivery fee, which you will see clearly at checkout before making a payment. Our standard fee is typically around â‚¹30.

Once you place an order, you can track its status in the "My Orders" section of your customer dashboard. You will receive real-time updates from the moment the shop accepts your order to when it's out for delivery.

Return policies are set by the individual shops. Generally, perishable items like groceries and vegetables cannot be returned unless they are of poor quality. For other items, you can initiate a return request from your "My Orders" page within the shop's specified return window. Refunds will be processed once the shop confirms receipt of the returned item.

If you have an issue with your order, the fastest way is to contact the shop directly using the details on the order page. For platform-related issues or other queries, you can reach our customer support team via the "Contact Us" page or email us at support@localconnect24x7.com.
